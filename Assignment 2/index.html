E-commerce Record During the Pandemic
United States of America

Has the Pandemic changed the way we shop ?
Consumer behaviours have changed drastically since COVID-19. (2020) More and more people are shifting to online shopping. E-commerce is growing at a fast rate.

E-commerce Boom

Geographically, it is clear that major states had higher order numbers. For example: New York, Texas, California, Washington.

Over time, we can see a surge in online orders. In March, when lockdown starts, a sharp increase in e-commerce sales. As expected, sales went up the last quarter of the year due to holiday. The number of orders was the highest in September.

Change in Demand

Average consumer was still the number one customer segment. However, as the pandemic kept people at home to work and study, Home Office rose to become a big customer segment. Consequently, majority of orders was Office Supplies .

Saving costs while shopping online

Even though spending on online purchases increased, people still chose to save cost through shipping costs. Standard Class shipment method was the most popular choice, while Same Day was the least popular.
